DIY RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

100 gm, Sunscreen base

0.5gm, Aloe vera gel

0.3 gm, Jojoba oil

0.3 gm, White Tea, and Aloe fragrance

RECIPE

Step : 1

In a wide-mouth sanitised container, weigh and add 100 grams of Soapy Twist Gel Sunscreen Base (SPF 30)

Step : 2

add 0.5 grams of Aloe Vera Gel, 0.3 grams of Jojoba Oil and O.3 grams of White Tea and Aloe fragrance oil in a separate sanitized bowl. Mix well to ensure the ingredients blend in fully.

Step : 3

Add this paste to your cream base and mix well till the paste is infused completely.

Step : 4

Store in a sanitized airtight container and keep it away from direct sun, at room temperature.

BESTBEFORE

For best results use within 12 months.
